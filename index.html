    About

    Code for Future is a programme which is going to be organised by IEEE JADAVPUR UNIVERSITY STUDENT'S BRANCH uniting the members and representatives of various IEEE student branches across India, as well as numerous enterprising pupils too. It is a chance for everyone to engage in thought-provoking discussions on technological stacks, participate in industrial application tracks and also learn the importance of IEEE in industrial and academic level and ultimately making a better future. This event marks the spirit of cognition and research.Special thanks to All India IEEE Computer Society Student & Young Professional Congress
